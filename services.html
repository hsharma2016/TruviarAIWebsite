Getting Started

From Sign-Off to Full
Pipeline in 30 Days

1

Discovery Call (Day 1)

A free, no-obligation 30-minute session. We map your ideal client profile, audit your current outreach, and define your appointment criteria — deal size, decision-maker seniority, sector focus, and timeline expectations. You'll leave with clarity, regardless of whether we work together.

2

System Build (Days 2–7)

Our team builds your complete outbound infrastructure: verified prospect database, email domain warm-up and technical configuration, personalised copy sequences, LinkedIn outreach cadences, and CRM pipeline setup. You're briefed at each stage — nothing is deployed without your sign-off.

3

Campaign Launch (Day 8)

Outreach begins. All responses are monitored and handled by our team. Every reply is screened against your qualification criteria before we book anything into your calendar. You focus on your clients — we handle your pipeline.

4

Appointments Land (Weeks 2–4)

Qualified meetings appear on your calendar — complete with prospect background, company intelligence, and conversation notes so you walk into every call prepared and in control. Most clients see their first booked meeting within 14 days of launch.

5

Monthly Review & Scale (Ongoing)

A transparent performance report, a 30-minute strategy review call, and a forward plan for the next 30 days. We share what's working, optimise what isn't, and scale campaigns as your capacity grows. You gain a predictable pipeline — permanently.
