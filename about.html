The Story

Why I Started Truviar

The Pattern I Kept Seeing

Working alongside consulting firms — both as a client and as a collaborator — I noticed the same painful pattern everywhere. Brilliant experts. Impressive credentials. Transformational results for clients. And yet, a constant, grinding anxiety about where the next project would come from.

The feast-or-famine cycle isn't a business failure. It's an engineering failure. The system for acquiring clients is broken, inconsistent, or non-existent. And unlike a failed process in a factory floor, the consequences don't show up immediately — they compound quietly over months and years.

The Solution Was Obvious (To An Engineer)

Any sufficiently complex problem can be solved by breaking it into variables, controlling those variables, and systematically improving outcomes. B2B client acquisition is complex — but it's not magic.

I founded Truviar AI Consulting to bring a precision-engineering mindset to outbound sales for consulting firms. To build the system they never had time to build themselves. And to back it with a guarantee that keeps us accountable — the same way every contract I delivered for GM, Ford, and BMW did.
